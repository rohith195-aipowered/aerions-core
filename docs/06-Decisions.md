# Architectural Decisions

This document records permanent architectural decisions.

---

## Decision 001

Aerions is an Intelligence Operating Layer.

It is not an AI application.

---

## Decision 002

Objectives are more important than prompts.

The platform understands intent rather than prompt wording.

---

## Decision 003

Models are interchangeable.

No provider becomes a permanent dependency.

---

## Decision 004

Software should always be preferred over AI whenever deterministic execution is possible.

---

## Decision 005

Planning must occur before execution.

---

## Decision 006

Every component should have one responsibility.

---

## Decision 007

Memory belongs to Aerions, not individual AI providers.

---

## Decision 008

Architecture should remain stable even as models evolve.

---

## Decision 009

Every execution should be observable and explainable.

Aerions should always know why a decision was made.

---

## Decision 010

Pristelle is the first production customer of Aerions.

Real-world usage should drive future architectural improvements.
